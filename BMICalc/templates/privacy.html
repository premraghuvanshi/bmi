Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the BMI Calculator & Health Instruction System. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal health data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

To provide accurate health insights, we may collect the following data points:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, Email address (for account creation).
  • Health Metrics: Height, Weight, Age, Gender, and calculated BMI history.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited and interaction with health tips.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly for:

  • Calculating your Body Mass Index (BMI).
  • Generating personalized diet and exercise suggestions.
  • Tracking your progress over time via charts.

Note: We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure server storage, to protect your health information from unauthorized access.
